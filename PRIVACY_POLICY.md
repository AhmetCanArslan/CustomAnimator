# Privacy Policy

## Overview

Custom Animator is designed with privacy as a priority. This app does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## Data Collection

Custom Animator does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics. All data remains local on your device.

## Permissions

The app requires the following permission to function:

- **WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS**: Allows the app to modify system animation scale settings on your device.

This permission is necessary to adjust animation configurations and is not used for any other purpose.

## Local Storage

Your saved animation presets are stored locally on your device and are never shared, transmitted, or backed up to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if the app's functionality changes. Users will be notified of any significant changes.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the app store listing.
